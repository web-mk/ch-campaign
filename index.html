The new center will offer ample space for various age and interest groups to gather comfortably in their own designated spaces. The expansive layout will allow multiple activities to take place simultaneously without infringing upon each other.

Imagine a vibrant Jewish hub where teens are practicing dance moves, women are discussing a new book, a study group is poring over the Talmud, a foursome is playing mahjong, and little ones are singing Hebrew songs. All at the same time! What a beautiful tapestry of Jewish life!

A spacious sanctuary, with flex-seating up to 240, will offer a warm and welcoming setting, reflecting the diversity of the families who call Chabad their home.

Our large, well-appointed social hall, with seating for 250, will serve as the venue for the weekly Shabbat luncheon, cultural events, educational seminars, and lifecycle occasions where families come together to celebrate, connect, and engage.
